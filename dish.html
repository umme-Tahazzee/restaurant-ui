A Florentine classic elevated to its peak expression. Our T-bone is dry-aged for 28 days in our in-house aging room, then seared over a custom wood-fire grill at 900°F for a perfectly caramelized crust. Finished tableside with rosemary-infused compound butter and whole roasted garlic cloves.

Chef Marco's Note

"This is the dish I cook when I want to show someone the soul of Italian cooking — no shortcuts, no garnishes to hide behind. Just exceptional beef and fire."
